Private Dining & Events

Brodeto offers an intimate setting for your most important celebrations. Whether you're hosting an engagement dinner, wedding rehearsal, corporate gathering, or private celebration, our culinary expertise and thoughtful service create a memorable experience.

Private Dining Room

Capacity: 24 Guests

Our dedicated private dining room provides an exclusive environment for your event. Chef Scott Crawford will work with you to create a menu that reflects your vision and celebration.

Minimum: $3,000 – $5,000

Full Restaurant Buyout

Capacity: Up to 80 Guests

For larger celebrations, we offer a complete restaurant buyout. This allows for full customization and the ability to create a truly private event experience.

Minimum: $12,000 – $20,000

Service Styles

Family-Style Service

Dishes are presented family-style, allowing guests to serve themselves from shared platters. This creates an intimate, communal dining experience perfect for celebrations.

Plated Service

Chef Crawford crafts an elegant multi-course tasting menu with each course plated individually. This formal service style allows for culinary precision and visual presentation.

Perfect For

Rehearsal dinners • Engagement celebrations • Wedding receptions • Corporate dinners • Private celebrations • Anniversary dinners • Birthday parties • Family reunions
