Directions

Brodeto is located at Raleigh Iron Works, a historic industrial building in downtown Raleigh. From I-40, take the downtown exit and follow signs to Iron Works. Complimentary deck parking is available.

Parking

Complimentary parking is available in Raleigh Iron Works' deck. Validated parking for guests. Street parking is also available in the surrounding area.

Special Requests & Dietary Needs

Please mention any dietary restrictions or allergies when making your reservation. Our kitchen is happy to accommodate vegetarian, gluten-free, and other dietary needs. Call us at (919) 615-0388 to discuss your requirements.
