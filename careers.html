Careers at Brodeto - Fine Dining Jobs in Raleigh, NC

Join Our Team

Brodeto is seeking passionate hospitality professionals who share our commitment to culinary excellence and refined service. We believe in fostering a supportive environment where team members can grow and thrive.

Why Work at Brodeto

At Brodeto, we celebrate the craft of fine dining. Our team is comprised of dedicated professionals who understand the importance of attention to detail, guest satisfaction, and culinary integrity. We invest in our people through training, mentorship, and career development opportunities.

Current Positions

Sous Chef

We are seeking an experienced Sous Chef to support Chef Scott Crawford in the kitchen. The ideal candidate has strong culinary skills, experience in fine dining, and a passion for Adriatic cuisine. Responsibilities include coordinating kitchen operations, training staff, and ensuring consistency in food preparation.

Line Cook

Join our kitchen team as a Line Cook. We are looking for motivated individuals with experience in a fine dining environment. Strong knife skills, attention to detail, and a commitment to quality are essential. This is an excellent opportunity to develop your skills under the mentorship of experienced chefs.

Front of House Manager

We seek an experienced Front of House Manager to oversee dining room operations. The successful candidate will have strong leadership skills, experience in upscale dining, and a genuine passion for hospitality. Responsibilities include managing reservations, coordinating service, and ensuring guest satisfaction.

Server

Experienced servers are needed to deliver exceptional service to our guests. The ideal candidate has knowledge of fine dining practices, wine knowledge, and strong communication skills. You will work in an intimate 24-seat dining room and play a key role in creating memorable experiences for our guests.

Sommelier

We are looking for a passionate Sommelier to manage our wine program. The ideal candidate has extensive wine knowledge, particularly Italian and Croatian selections, and can guide guests through our curated wine list. This role offers the opportunity to work with high-quality wines in an intimate setting.
