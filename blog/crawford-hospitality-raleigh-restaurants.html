Chef Scott Crawford's Raleigh Restaurant Family

Published by Brodeto | June 2025

Chef Scott Crawford has spent years building a unique collection of restaurants in Raleigh that showcase different culinary perspectives and dining experiences. Rather than a single vision, Crawford Hospitality represents a philosophy of excellence across multiple cuisines and dining styles. Located primarily at the iconic Raleigh Iron Works in downtown Raleigh, each restaurant maintains its own identity while sharing a commitment to quality, craftsmanship, and genuine hospitality.

What makes Crawford Hospitality distinctive is the diversity of experiences available within a shared community. Whether you're seeking the delicate flavors of the Adriatic, the refined comfort of French bistro cuisine, the boldness of New Southern traditions, innovative cocktails in an underground speakeasy, or the timeless appeal of a premium steakhouse, Crawford's family of restaurants offers something exceptional for every palate and occasion.

Brodeto: Adriatic Italian & Croatian Seafood

Brodeto stands as Chef Crawford's flagship restaurant, dedicated to the seafood traditions of the Adriatic region. Drawing inspiration from the coasts of Italy and Croatia, Brodeto celebrates fresh ingredients prepared with traditional techniques that honor the sea's bounty. The restaurant's commitment to authenticity—from ingredient sourcing to preparation methods—makes it Raleigh's premier destination for Adriatic cuisine. Visit Brodeto's website to explore the menu and make your reservation.

Jolie: French Bistro Excellence

Jolie brings authentic French bistro dining to Raleigh's restaurant scene, offering classic dishes prepared with the precision and care that define French culinary traditions. From duck confit to perfectly prepared seafood, Jolie captures the warmth and sophistication of Parisian bistros. The restaurant serves as a gathering place for those who appreciate refined comfort food and excellent wine selections. Learn more at Jolie's website.

Bar Sous Terre: Underground Cocktail Bar

Located beneath Jolie, Bar Sous Terre is an intimate underground cocktail bar that brings craft mixology to downtown Raleigh. The speakeasy-style atmosphere, combined with expertly crafted cocktails and knowledgeable bartenders, creates a sophisticated space for evening drinks and conversation. Bar Sous Terre represents Chef Crawford's commitment to hospitality beyond food, extending into the full dining experience. Find more information at Jolie's website.

Crawford & Son: New Southern Cuisine

Crawford & Son celebrates the rich traditions of New Southern cooking, blending classic Southern techniques with contemporary sensibilities and premium ingredients. This restaurant showcases the diversity of Southern cuisine—from coastal seafood preparations to smoked and grilled specialties. Crawford & Son appeals to those seeking familiar flavors refined through skilled preparation and thoughtful menu development. Discover more at Crawford & Son's website.

Crawford Brothers: Premium Steakhouse

Crawford Brothers brings sophisticated steakhouse dining to Raleigh, combining premium beef selections with craft cocktails and an impressive wine list. The restaurant represents a return to steakhouse traditions—quality meat, skilled preparation, and attentive service in a refined atmosphere. Whether for a special occasion or a celebration of fine dining, Crawford Brothers stands as a destination for those who appreciate excellence in its most classic form. Visit Crawford Brothers' website for more information.

Crawford Hospitality: A Shared Vision

Crawford Hospitality serves as the parent organization for this collection of restaurants, united by shared values of quality, integrity, and genuine hospitality. While each restaurant maintains its own distinct character and culinary focus, they all reflect Chef Crawford's deep commitment to excellence and respect for culinary traditions. Located together at Raleigh Iron Works in downtown Raleigh, these restaurants form a vibrant community dedicated to providing Raleigh with exceptional dining experiences across multiple cuisines and styles. Learn more about Crawford Hospitality at their website.
