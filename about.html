About Brodeto

Brodeto is the embodiment of Chef Scott Crawford's lifelong passion for Adriatic cuisine. After years of exploring the coastal regions of Italy and Croatia, Chef Crawford created Brodeto to share the authentic flavors and techniques of the Mediterranean with Raleigh.

The name Brodeto comes from a traditional Adriatic fish stew—a symbol of the region's seafaring heritage and commitment to fresh, seasonal ingredients. Every element of Brodeto reflects this dedication: from our carefully curated wine program featuring Italian and Croatian selections to our ever-changing menu based on the finest available seafood.

Chef Scott Crawford

Chef Scott Crawford has spent decades mastering the culinary traditions of Italy and Croatia. His approach to cooking is rooted in respect for ingredient quality, traditional preparation methods, and an understanding of how flavors balance and complement each other across a meal.

At Brodeto, Chef Crawford offers a refined yet approachable dining experience—a place where sophisticated cuisine celebrates simplicity and the natural beauty of fresh seafood. This is fine dining that honors tradition while embracing the possibilities of contemporary technique.

The Adriatic Vision

The Adriatic Sea spans centuries of culinary tradition, connecting communities across the coasts of Italy, Croatia, Slovenia, and beyond. These regions share a deep cultural heritage centered on the bounty of the sea, the seasonality of ingredients, and the communal joy of sharing a meal.

Brodeto invites you to experience this tradition in an intimate setting. Our 24-seat dining room creates the feeling of being welcomed into a private dining club, where every guest receives attention and care befitting their importance.
